Each year the LBRN program has an annual meeting in which program participants, committee members and administrators meet to review individual research accomplishments and to discuss the overall program activity. Summer research faculty and graduate and undergraduate students are encouraged to present their LBRN sponsored research, and talks are scheduled to highlight sponsored research projects from partnered campuses across the state. This year's meeting is being held on January 30 - Februrary 1, 2015 at the Baton Rouge Marriott.

Presenters: INBRE Project PIs, and Pilot Project PIs, 2014 LBRN Summer Faculty, and Graduate Students are required to submit an abstract to participate during the LBRN 13th Annual Meeting. Abstract submission form will be sent after your registration.

Posters: Participants are invited to submit a poster at the meeting. Poster abstract submission form will be sent after your registration.
