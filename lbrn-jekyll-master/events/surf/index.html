About SURF

Pete Maravich Assembly Center - Concourse, August 1, 2014

The 21st Annual Summer Undergraduate Research Forum (SURF) on August 1, 2014 from 1-4 PM, at the Pete Maravich Assembly Center - Concourse, is pleased to showcase the numerous undergraduate students who have engaged in summer research projects this year at institutions throughout the LSU System.

SURF participants represent programs, including these:

  • NIH LSU/BRCC Bridges to the Baccalaureate Program (Bridges)
  • Center for Computation & Technology Research Experiences for Undergraduates (CCT REU)
  • HHMI Professor's Program (HHMI Professors)
  • Initiative for Maximizing Student Development Program (IMSD)
  • LSU-Howard Hughes Medical Institute (HHMI) Program
  • Louisiana Alliance for Simulation-Guided Materials Applications (LA-SiGMA)
  • Louisiana Biomedical Research Network (LBRN)
  • Louisiana Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics Research Scholars Program (LA-STEM)
  • McNair Research Scholar Program (McNair)
  • Robert Noyce Scholarship Program (Geaux Teach NOYCE)
  • Office of Strategic Initiatives Research Experiences for Undergraduate (OSI REU)
  • Physics & Astronomy Research Experiences for Undergraduate (P&A REU)
  • Supervised Undergraduate Research Experience (SURE)
  • and Individual student researchers in various laboratories across LSU
